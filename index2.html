🍝 Creamy Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until tender.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for one minute.
  4. Pour in the cream and stir well.
  5. Add Parmesan cheese and mix until smooth.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add the cooked pasta and stir until coated.
  8. Serve hot and garnish with parsley.

Cooking Information

Preparation Time 10 minutes
Cooking Time 20 minutes
Total Time 30 minutes
Servings 4 people

Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)

Nutrient Amount
Calories 420 kcal
Protein 14 g
Carbohydrates 48 g
